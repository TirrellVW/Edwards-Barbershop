Craft You Can

Eddie at Edwards Barber Shop has been a cornerstone of the Glen Cove community for over 16 years. He's not just a barber — he's a craftsman who studies each client individually, learning their hair, their style, and their story.

Clients travel from across Long Island for his signature straight-razor shave and precision cuts. Once you sit in Eddie's chair, you understand why.
